About Us

Mike Pearman

Owner and Founder of Integrity Restoration Stl

Integrity Restoration performs complete home remodeling services for interior and exterior projects. Our areas of expertise include roofing, deck replacement, kitchen and bathroom remodels, and much more. With over 15 years of experience in remodeling and restoration projects, Integrity Restoration can help with any of your project needs!

Call us at (314)640-1848 or email mapintegrity@gmail.com for more information.

Integrity Restoration was created in 2003 with one man and a truck. The company has grown into a crew of four hardworking men, capable of managing multiple projects across the St. Louis area. Owner of the company, Mike Pearman, offers opportunities to rehabilitated, young men looking for a fresh start. Mike provides the necessary technical training to his crew members, and offers his experience on leading a healthy, productive lifestyle.
